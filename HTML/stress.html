Stress

On this page, you can find articles, videos, and take a survey related to stress to help manage it better.

Remedies

1. Practice deep breathing exercises.

2. Engage in physical activities like yoga or walking.

3. Take short breaks from work to relax and unwind.

Causes of Stress

1. Work-related pressure.

2. Major life changes or personal issues.

3. Financial difficulties or uncertainty.
